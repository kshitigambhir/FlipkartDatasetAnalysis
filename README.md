# 📊 Flipkart Product Analysis

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Flipkart product data to extract meaningful insights related to pricing, customer ratings, and product categories. The analysis helps understand customer behavior and product trends.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib (Data Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📁 Dataset

- Flipkart product dataset (train.csv)
- Contains ~15,000+ records with features like price, rating, category, and review counts

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

- Converted price and discount columns into numeric format  
- Removed missing/null values  
- Standardized column names  
- Handled inconsistent data types

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Women category has the highest number of products, indicating higher product variety  
- Men category has a higher average price, suggesting premium pricing  
- Price distribution is right-skewed, meaning most products are in the lower price range  
- Weak positive correlation (~0.29) between price and rating  
- Expensive products generally maintain high ratings  
- Majority of ratings are 4-star and 5-star, indicating positive customer satisfaction  

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis shows that most products on the platform are budget-friendly, while premium products also maintain strong customer satisfaction. Pricing alone does not determine ratings, suggesting other factors like quality and brand value influence customer reviews.
